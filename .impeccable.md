## Design Context

### Users
Internal insurance agents and support reps at Libra Insurance (ליברה ביטוח). They look up policy and claims information quickly during live customer calls or chats. Speed of information retrieval is critical — they need to find the right answer fast under pressure.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, calm, clear. Libra is a serious insurance brand: reliable, no surprises, easy to understand. The yellow (#ffd703) brand color is the primary accent.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Clean white background with yellow (#ffd703) accent. RTL Hebrew interface. System UI font stack for cross-platform reliability. No decorative flourishes — clarity and speed over style.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity first**: Every element should serve information retrieval. No decoration that slows scanning.
2. **Consistent Hebrew**: All user-facing text must be in Hebrew. No English category names or labels in the UI.
3. **Accessible interactions**: Interactive elements must have keyboard focus states, not just hover states.
4. **Performance**: Internal tools load fast. No heavy fonts or unnecessary assets.
5. **Quality bar: MVP** — functionally complete and reliable is the goal. Polish focuses on fixing bugs and inconsistencies, not visual ambition.
